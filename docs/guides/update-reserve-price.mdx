# Update reserve price

Creators are able to update the reserve price of their NFT up until the point of the reserve price being met.

If your reserve price has been met you will not be able to change the price.

- How to update your reserve price:

  - Click onto the MyNftsList page and there is a 'Update' next to the transfer button

  - Click "Update"

  - Enter the updated reserve price

  - Finalize your price by clicking "CONFIRM"

  - When clicking "CONFIRM", the prices will be updated after the blocks are synchronized.
