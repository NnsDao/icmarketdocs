# How to cancel your listing

“Buy Now” listings can be cancelled at anytime up until the sale has taken place.

Once a sale has taken place it cannot be cancelled or reversed.

- How to cancel your listing:

  - Click onto the MyNftsList page

  - Click the “Update” button

  - ![icmarket guide](https://docs.icmarket.app/imgs/media/16487129597035/16488838855931.jpg)

  - Select the “Cancel listing” button

  - Confirm you're wanting to cancel your listing by clicking “Cancel listing”

  - Your dialog will prompt you to “confirm” a transaction to complete the process.

  - You will see a 'Cancel Listing Success!' to confirm your listing has been cancelled.
