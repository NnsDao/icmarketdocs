# Selling NFTs

### 1.Go to your account page,Find your NFT on your collection page

![icmarket guide](https://docs.icmarket.app/imgs/media/16487129899721/16487208470712.jpg)

### 2. Click on “sell” a modal will appear. You will need to select which currency you would like to sell with. You can sell with either NDP or ICP.

![icmarket guide](https://docs.icmarket.app/imgs/media/16487129899721/16487209098710.jpg)

## 3.Adjust your selling price

![icmarket guide](https://docs.icmarket.app/imgs/media/16487129899721/16487208677608.jpg)

You can click 'update' to adjust your selling price
